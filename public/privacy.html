Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

The short version: Cutout removes image backgrounds using an AI model that runs entirely inside your web browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers or anyone else's. They stay on your device.

1. Images You Process

When you use Cutout, the background-removal happens locally on your device using WebAssembly/WebGPU. The image you select is loaded into your browser's memory, processed there, and the result is offered to you for download. At no point is your image transmitted to Cutout, Syncster, or any third party. We do not see, store, collect, or have any access to your images.

2. Information We Collect

Cutout itself does not require an account and does not ask for any personal information. We do not operate a database of users. The limited data involved is:

  • Standard server logs: our hosting/CDN may record basic technical data (IP address, browser type, pages requested, timestamps) for security and reliability. This is standard for any website.
  • The AI model files: the first time you use Cutout, your browser downloads the AI model from a content delivery network (staticimgly.com) and caches it. This is a normal file download, not an upload of your data.

Cutout is free and supported by advertising through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.
  • For more choices about third-party vendor cookies, visit aboutads.info.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting, aggregate analytics to understand overall traffic (for example, how many people visit). Any such analytics measure trends in aggregate and are not used to identify you personally, and never involve your images.

5. Children's Privacy

Cutout is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
